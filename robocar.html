Autonomous RC Car Platform

A full-stack autonomous vehicle system developed from scratch, featuring custom computer vision and control algorithms.

Project Overview

As AI Lead, I architected and implemented the complete autonomy stack for this modified RC car, including:

  • Modular AI System: Separated perception and control for transparency
  • Custom Simulation: Training environment developed from scratch
  • Embedded Deployment: Optimized for real-time performance
  • Hardware Integration: Custom 3D-printed mounting solutions
