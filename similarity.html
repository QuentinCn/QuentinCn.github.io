Similarity Score

Since we were proposing more ecological alternative to a fast fashion article we needed to sort them by similarity in order to propose something relatively looking like the original article choseen by the user

So I worked on a similarity score AI

Basically it is an IA capable of detecting the type of article but also the color and some shape on it.
Using those prediction, i compute the cosine similarity and obtain a percentage of similarity
