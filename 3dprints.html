Custom 3D Printed Components

Project Overview

During the vehicle's electrical system overhaul, I designed and manufactured custom mounting solutions to securely integrate new components while maintaining accessibility and serviceability.

Key Components

VESC Motor Controller Bracket

A vibration-resistant mount with integrated cable routing channels for the Vedder Electronic Speed Controller.

Modular Battery Enclosure

Lightweight protective casing featuring ventilation channels and quick-release mounting points.

Electronics Mainboard

Central mounting platform for the Jetson Nano AI computer and power conversion modules, designed with service access in mind.

Adjustable Camera Mount

Precision mount allowing ±15° of camera angle adjustment with secure locking mechanism.

Design & Manufacturing Process

CAD Modeling

  • Used FreeCAD for parametric 3D modeling
  • Iterative design process with functional prototypes
  • DFM (Design for Manufacturing) considerations for FDM printing

Production

  • Sliced with PrusaSlicer using optimized profiles
  • Printed in PLA and PLA+
  • Post-processing including drilling and thread inserts
