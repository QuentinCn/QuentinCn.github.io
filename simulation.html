Custom Simulation Environment for AI Training

When I began developing my AI models, I realized the importance of a robust training environmentâ€”especially for reinforcement learning (RL), where real-world testing would be impractical (and risky for the vehicle).

I explored existing simulators but found them overly complex for my needs, with excessive dependencies and features tailored for multi-lane traffic systems. I required a lightweight solution focused solely on lane detection, with simple white lane markers and minimal distractions.

This led me to develop a custom simulator specifically designed to:

  • Train RL agents in a risk-free virtual environment
  • Generate large, diverse datasets for lane detection systems
  • Test different camera configurations (height, angle, etc.) systematically
